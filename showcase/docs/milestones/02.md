# Showcase Milestone 2 — Generated Asset Conventions, Title, and Dual-VDP Compositing

Objective:
- Lock the generated-asset approach and deliver the identity and compositing
  scenes as the first coherent verification-facing visuals.

Required docs:
- `docs/spec/00-overview.md`
- `docs/spec/02-video.md`
- `showcase/docs/showcase-rom-plan.md`
- `showcase/docs/showcase-implementation-plan.md`

Allowed scope:
- Generated asset guidelines, provenance notes, title/identity scene,
  compositing scene, and checkpoint metadata tied to those scenes.

Explicit non-goals:
- Tile-mode, sprite-capability, mixed-mode, command-engine, audio, or system
  validation scenes.
- Elaborate art polish that reduces verification readability.

Allowed paths:
- `showcase/`

Required verification:
- Title scene presents a stable identity screen.
- Compositing scene makes VDP-A transparency and VDP-B reveal behavior obvious.
- Generated asset provenance and conversion assumptions are recorded in docs.

Exit criteria:
- A reviewer can identify title and compositing checkpoints visually and via
  documented headless captures.

Forbidden extras:
- Any reliance on text modes, unverified scroll, or non-covered video behavior.

Verification commands:
```bash
python3 showcase/tools/package/build_showcase.py
python3 showcase/tools/package/run_showcase_headless.py --frames 480 --trace build/showcase/m2.trace --hash-frame 479 --symbols build/showcase/showcase.sym
```
